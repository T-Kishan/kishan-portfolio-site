VHSearch is a full-stack AI-powered email assistant developed as part of an AI Innovation Challenge hosted by the Singapore Chinese Chamber of Commerce & Industry (SCCCI). As part of the challenge, our team was matched with a real-world client: VHA Consultants Pte. Ltd., an engineering consultancy based in Singapore.

VHA, known for their MEP consultancy and project management services, expressed the need for a smarter way to sort and manage high volumes of email. VHSearch was built to address that by helping to streamline workflows using automation and generative AI.
